Risk Engine

Built To Stop Bad Decisions Before They Become Expensive.

Most account damage does not come from one planned loss. It comes from the decisions after that loss: revenge trades, oversized entries, moving stops, ignoring news, and trying to recover quickly. VEXTRON risk tools are designed to bring the trader back to structure.

Daily kill switch

Set a point where trading should stop for the day after a defined loss, poor execution, or emotional behavior.

Monthly guardrails

Track drawdown and performance limits so one bad period does not destroy a full trading plan.

Pre-trade risk check

Review stop distance, exposure, expected target, and trade quality before committing capital.

Discipline reminders

Keep the trader focused on planned execution instead of late entries, overtrading, or emotional recovery attempts.
