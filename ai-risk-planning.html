Protection

Good Trading Starts With Knowing What You Can Lose.

Most traders focus on profit first. Professional risk planning starts with the opposite question: where is the idea wrong, how much account exposure is acceptable, and does the potential reward justify the risk?

VEXTRON helps traders think through stop placement, trade size, volatility, and market structure before execution. This does not remove market risk, but it creates a more disciplined process.

Stop Logic

Invalidation matters

A stop loss should sit where the trade idea is no longer valid, not where emotion feels comfortable.

Sizing

Control exposure

Lot size should reflect account risk, stop distance, and volatility instead of confidence or urgency.

Reward

Respect the ratio

Targets should make sense compared with the risk taken, market structure, and realistic movement potential.
