1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you interact with VEXTRON, we may collect the following categories of information:

  • Contact information: name, email address, social media handle, Telegram handle, and messages you send to us.
  • Account and access information: login details, subscription status, plan selection, service requests, support history, and authentication-related records.
  • Trading workflow information: calculator inputs, risk preferences, trade planning notes, P/L book entries, performance review details, market interests, and setup review requests if you choose to provide them.
  • Payment and billing information: plan, transaction status, invoice records, and billing identifiers. Payment card or banking information may be handled by third-party payment processors and is not intended to be stored directly by VEXTRON unless clearly stated.
  • Technical information: IP address, device type, browser type, operating system, approximate location, referral source, page visits, timestamps, and diagnostic logs.
  • Communication information: emails, support messages, collaboration requests, feedback, suggestions, and responses to VEXTRON communications.

2. How We Use Information

VEXTRON may use collected information to:

  • provide, operate, maintain, and improve VEXTRON services, pages, tools, calculators, analysis, and user workflows;
  • respond to support requests, questions, signal-access inquiries, suggestions, collaboration requests, and account issues;
  • deliver service updates, market-related communications, platform notices, educational material, and important security or policy messages;
  • process subscriptions, plan access, billing records, payments, refunds, and service administration;
  • understand how users interact with VEXTRON and improve usability, reliability, content quality, and platform performance;
  • protect VEXTRON, users, and services from misuse, fraud, spam, unauthorized access, security threats, and policy violations;
  • comply with applicable legal, regulatory, accounting, tax, dispute-resolution, or enforcement obligations.

3. Trading And Platform Data

VEXTRON may offer or discuss tools for margin calculation, profit/loss estimation, risk planning, trade setup review, kill switch logic, monthly P/L tracking, market analysis, market updates, event awareness, and related trading workflows. If users enter trading-related information into VEXTRON systems, we use it to provide the requested tool, calculation, feature, support, or service.

Trading-related data can be sensitive because it may reveal strategy, preferences, risk appetite, trading frequency, or performance. VEXTRON aims to treat this information with care and does not sell individual trading records. Users should avoid submitting information they do not want processed or reviewed for service delivery.

4. Cookies, Analytics, And Similar Technologies

VEXTRON may use cookies, pixels, local storage, analytics tools, and similar technologies to keep services functioning, remember preferences, understand site performance, measure engagement, detect misuse, and improve user experience. Some tools may be provided by third parties such as analytics, hosting, security, communication, or advertising providers.

Users may control cookies through browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect website functionality, login behavior, personalization, analytics, or access to certain platform features.

5. How We Share Information

VEXTRON does not sell personal information as a core business model. We may share information in limited situations, including:

  • Service providers: hosting, email, analytics, payment, security, storage, customer support, automation, and communication providers that help operate VEXTRON.
  • Payment processors: providers that process transactions, subscription payments, refunds, billing records, fraud prevention, and payment verification.
  • Professional advisors: lawyers, accountants, auditors, consultants, or compliance advisors when needed for legitimate business purposes.
  • Legal and safety reasons: when required by law, court order, government request, dispute process, rights protection, fraud prevention, or security investigation.
  • Business transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, restructuring, financing, asset sale, or similar business transaction, subject to appropriate confidentiality protections.
  • With user direction: when you ask us to share information, connect a workflow, or communicate with a third party on your behalf.

6. Data Security

VEXTRON uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. These safeguards may include access controls, account protections, secure service providers, HTTPS-enabled transmission, internal restrictions, and monitoring for misuse.

No website, platform, email system, payment workflow, or internet-based service can guarantee absolute security. Users are responsible for keeping passwords secure, using trusted devices, avoiding suspicious links, and contacting VEXTRON if they suspect unauthorized account activity.

7. Data Retention

VEXTRON retains information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide services, maintain account access, support users, keep business records, improve platform reliability, resolve disputes, prevent misuse, and satisfy legal, tax, accounting, or compliance obligations.

Retention periods may vary based on the type of data, the reason it was collected, user requests, legal requirements, backup systems, fraud-prevention needs, and operational requirements. When information is no longer needed, VEXTRON will delete, anonymize, or restrict it where reasonably practical.

8. User Rights And Choices

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, portability, withdrawal of consent, or information about how personal data is processed. You may also have rights to opt out of certain marketing communications or specific data-sharing activities.

To make a privacy request, contact VEXTRON at hello@vextron.pro. We may need to verify your identity before responding. Some requests may be limited by legal, security, fraud-prevention, accounting, or operational requirements.

Marketing communications

You may unsubscribe from promotional emails where an unsubscribe option is provided, or contact us to request removal from non-essential marketing communications. We may still send transactional, security, billing, account, policy, or service-related messages.

9. International Users

VEXTRON may serve users across different countries. Information may be processed in countries other than where the user is located, including locations where our hosting providers, service providers, contractors, or business operations are based. Data protection laws may differ between jurisdictions.

Where required, VEXTRON aims to use appropriate safeguards for international processing and transfers, such as contractual restrictions, service-provider controls, data minimization, and security practices.
