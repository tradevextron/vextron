Timing

The Same Setup Can Behave Differently In A Different Session.

Market sessions shape liquidity, volatility, spread behavior, and speed. A setup during London open can behave very differently from the same structure during a quiet Asian range.

Session alerts are built to help traders prepare for active windows, avoid dead zones, and stay aware of high-risk periods around major data releases.

Asian Session

Range and preparation

Often useful for identifying levels, liquidity buildup, and quieter conditions before stronger movement appears.

London Session

Liquidity expansion

London can bring stronger movement in forex pairs, breakouts, reversals, and faster execution conditions.

New York Session

Continuation or reversal

New York often reacts to US data, dollar strength, equity sentiment, and London session structure.
