Refund Policy

Refunds For VEXTRON Subscriptions.

This Refund Policy explains how refund requests are handled for VEXTRON digital education plans and platform access.

Effective Date: July 1, 2026

1. Digital Access

VEXTRON provides digital access to market education resources, AI-assisted summaries, calculators, journals, and member tools. Once digital access has been activated or used, the service may not be returnable in the same way as a physical product.

2. Refund Window

You may request a refund within 7 days of purchase if you have not substantially used the member resources or dashboard access.

3. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds may be refused where access has been used extensively, account sharing or misuse is detected, a chargeback has already been opened, or the request violates payment processor rules.

4. How To Request A Refund

Email hello@vextron.pro with your account email, purchase date, and reason for the request. Approved refunds are returned through the original payment method where possible.

5. No Performance Guarantee

VEXTRON is an education and workflow platform. Payments do not guarantee income, market performance, account growth, or any financial outcome.
